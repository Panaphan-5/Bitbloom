✨ BitBloom - User Manual ✨

🌿 Overview

BitBloom is a habit tracking app that gamifies your daily routines. Users can either select habits manually or let the app surprise them with a random challenge. Completing habits increases your streak and unlocks badges. Skipping resets the streak.

📅 Home Tab

  • + Create your habit: Add a new habit with a title, description, and category.
  • Random your today's Habit: Let the app surprise you with a random habit to complete.
  • If completed, tap "✔️ Done!" to increase your streak.
  • If skipped, tap "Skip" to reset your streak to 0.

📝 Habits Tab

View all your created habits by category. Filter habits and track their individual progress.

✨ Badges Tab

Earn badges by completing specific actions such as:

  • Completing 10 days streak
  • Finishing habits in a specific category like "จิตใจ" or "พัฒนาตัวเอง"
  • Achieving overall habit completion goals

📊 Stats Tab

  • No-skip streak: Shows how many days in a row you've completed at least one habit without skipping.
  • Most achieved habit: Displays the most completed habit category today.
  • Most skipped habit: Displays the category you've skipped the most today.

⚙️ Settings Tab

  • Dark Mode Toggle: Switch between light and dark themes.
  • User Manual Link: Opens this guide in your default browser.

🔧 Technical Notes

  • Habits and streaks are persisted using @AppStorage.
  • Randomized habits are drawn from a remaining pool per day.
  • The app uses SwiftUI with MVVM architecture.
